Data Description:

X1: Amount of the given credit (NT dollar) 
X2: Gender (1 = male; 2 = female). 
X3: Education (1 = graduate school; 2 = university; 3 = high school; 4 = others). 
X4: Marital status (1 = married; 2 = single; 3 = others). 
X5: Age (years). 
X6 - X11: History of past payment. We tracked the past monthly payment records (from July to December 2017) as follows: 
X6 = the repayment status in December, 2017; X7 = the repayment status in November, 2017; . . .;X11 = the repayment status in July, 2017. 
The measurement scale for the repayment status is: -1 = pay duly; 1 = payment delay for one month; 2 = payment delay for two months; . . .; 8 = payment delay for eight months; 9 = payment delay for nine months and above. 
X12-X17: Amount of bill statement (NT dollar). X12 = amount of bill statement in December, 2017; X13 = amount of bill statement in November, 2017; . . .; X17 = amount of bill statement in July, 2017. 
X18-X23: Amount of previous payment (NT dollar). X18 = amount paid in December, 2017; X19 = amount paid in November, 2017; . . .;X23 = amount paid in July, 2017.

Y - Default Payment Predicted (1 = Default, 0 = Not Default)
